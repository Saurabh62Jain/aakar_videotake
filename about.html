About Aakar Videotake

"To gain recognition as a classic film producer."

Introduction

Establishment & Vision

Aakar Videotake was incorporated as a professional film production unit in 1987. Over the past several decades, we have established ourselves as a pioneer in storytelling, empanelled on the Outsource Producers Panel of Indian Doordarshan.

Our Core Activities:

  • 🎬 Cinema & Films
  • 📺 TV Software Programs
  • 🎥 Commercial Ad Films
  • 🎞️ Documentaries

In this creative journey, we have successfully produced over 23 Telefilms, 10 Documentaries, and numerous Ad-films. These works have been broadcasted nationwide on DDI (Doordarshan India), Doordarshan Bhopal, and other prestigious regional networks including Jaipur, Lucknow, Patna, and Jalandhar.

Beside this, Aakar Videotake works closely with multiple prestigious channels in the country. Our future pipeline includes multiple scriptwriting projects, with plans to produce a minimum of 3 films per year and expand national channel broadcasting schedules.

Our Legacy

Our Best Productions

A history of award-winning broadcasts and critically acclaimed cinematic projects.

1. Dharti Putra

1988

This telefilm was awarded three prestigious "Film Saurabh" awards (Best Direction, Best Cinematography, and Best Acting) by the Film Journalist Association of Madhya Pradesh in 1988. It was exhibited at the Madhya Pradesh Sarpanch Sammelan, drawing an audience of over 10,000 delegates from across the state.

2. Hum Jiyenge Aise

1989

Produced for public awareness and civic education, this telefilm was telecasted on the national network of Doordarshan to promote civic awareness and guide Indian citizens in all aspects of life.

3. Vandhan Apna

1989

A documentary focused on forest preservation, produced in collaboration with the Uttar Pradesh Chalchitra Vikas Nigam. The film was highly acclaimed and declared the Best Documentary Film of the Year 1989.

4. Addiant

1990

A feature film certified with a 'Universal' rating by the Indian Film Censor Board. The film's premier was inaugurated by the Hon'ble former Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Shri Arjun Singh.

5. Barood Ke Saudagar

1993

A landmark release in regional television, this production was the very first telefilm ever produced and broadcasted by Doordarshan Bhopal.

6. Badra Baras Gaye

1995

A critically acclaimed drama recognized as the Best Telefilm of the Year 1995 by Doordarshan Bhopal.

7. Tum Who Nahin

1997

Produced and telecasted in 1997, this telefilm was honored with the Best Cinematography Award by Doordarshan Bhopal for its outstanding visual language.

8. Aasara

1997

Produced specifically for the Raipur Doordarshan regional network, this project was awarded the Best Telefilm of the Year 1997.

9. Inhen Samhaliye

2000

An environmental documentary film advocating for forest preservation and demonstrating collaborative solutions for the joint management of forestlands.

10. Tapan

2013

A cinematic film produced under the prestigious Commissioned Category of Doordarshan India (DDI) in 2013.
